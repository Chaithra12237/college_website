    Women Cell

    Women Cell is a community of girl students in T K M College of Engineering, who share a common purpose to learn, educate and collaborate on ideas that have the power to effect meaningful and positive change while fighting the odds they face. It is an expanding network that brings them together to learn, laugh..

    Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Cell (IEDC)

    About IEDC The Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Cell of TKMCE is an organization working under the guidance of Kerala StartUp Mission that aims to promote the institutional vision of transforming youngsters into technological entrepreneurs and innovative leaders. The initiative is to address the rising trend of corporate job culture and establish a platform to pursue ideas.

    SAEINDIA TKMCE Collegiate Club

    SAEINDIA Collegiate Club – An official SAEINDIA student Club having a minimum of 50 student members, with a senior faculty Adviser and additional faculty Adviser, elected student club officers are squired to constitute a SAEINDIA Collegiate Club. SAEINDIA Collegiate Club of TKM College of Engineering consists of 2 faculty advisors and 86 student members.

    National Service Scheme (NSS)

    The National Service Scheme (NSS) is an All India Scheme initiated by the Union Ministry of Education and implemented at the colleges through the universities. This is an educational program in imparting education through Community Service. This helps the youth to build their character, maintain discipline, create a sense of dignity of labour.
